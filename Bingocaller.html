How to Play

  • Click Start (S) to begin a new game.
  • Optionally, click Select Cards to choose player cards for AutoCheck. Once a game starts, AutoCheck is locked — it cannot be changed mid-game.
  • Inside Select Cards, use the Player codes section to load cards directly from players. Tap the header to expand it, enter a player's share code (e.g. K4T-2BXQ) and tap + Add, or tap the scan button to scan a player's QR code directly with the camera — the code is added automatically. Cards are auto-selected and locked — they can only be removed by deleting that player entry. If two players share a card, both tags turn red and a clash warning appears.
  • Inside Select Cards, tap Options to expand settings. Toggle Confirm Win (on by default) to enable or disable manual win confirmation. Toggle AutoCheck to enable automatic win detection — this is locked once a game starts and cannot be changed mid-game. Choose which wins AutoCheck detects: Line, 2 Lines, and/or Full House (defaults: Line and Full House).
  • When AutoCheck is on and a win is detected, matching numbers on the grid turn yellow. The scrolling ticker at the top shows the current AutoCheck and Confirm Win status before a game starts.
  • When a win is detected and Confirm Win is on, a pulsing Confirm Win button appears in the bottom row. When a player shouts bingo, tap it to confirm — the win animation fires and the win text updates. If Next is pressed before confirming, the win opportunity expires silently.
  • If TTS Win State is enabled, Confirm Win is disabled — TTS handles announcing the win instead.
  • Click Next (Space) to call the next number. Click ↩ Undo (U) to undo the last number called — any pending or confirmed win for that number is also rolled back.
  • Type a card number into the Cards box to filter and select a card — its win status is shown, and its numbers highlight yellow on the grid.
  • Click End (E) to finish the game.
  • Tap ⚙ in the top right to open Settings. Toggle Sound, Text-to-speech, and Night mode. Press and hold the TTS row to open TTS settings — choose which items are read aloud (Number, Bingo Lingo, Win State) and drag ⠿ to change the order they are spoken.
  • If the Bingo Lingo text is visible and TTS is enabled, tap/click the text to have it read aloud.
  • Press and hold any feature button or label with a small ? indicator for a quick explanation of that feature.
