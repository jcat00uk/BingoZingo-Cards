How to Play

  • Click New Game to start a new game.
  • Use the Quick Pick buttons to automatically pick 1, 2, or 3 cards. Quick Pick 2 and Quick Pick 3 always pick cards with no repeated numbers between them.
  • Enable Big Card Mode (below the total cards count) before selecting a card to lock to a single large card display. In Big Card Mode only 1 card can be selected — Quick Pick 2 and 3 are disabled. On mobile, the card switches to landscape for easier reading.
  • Once you have selected cards, your share code appears below the Quick Pick buttons. Tap the code to copy it and share with your caller so they can load your cards into AutoCheck. Tap the QR button next to the code to show a fullscreen QR code your caller can scan directly.
  • By default only your selected cards are shown. Turn on Advanced Selection to browse and search all available cards manually.
  • In Advanced mode, cards are sorted by how many numbers they share with your current selection — fewer shared numbers appear first. Cards with 6 or more shared numbers are greyed out and blocked.
  • Cards showing an amber X shared label have some overlapping numbers — they can still be selected but some numbers will appear on more than one of your cards.
  • If a number appears on more than one of your cards, tapping it will automatically mark it on all your cards.
  • Click Confirm Selection to load your cards.
  • Tap 🎨 to change the card theme — pick a preset or customise the background, number, highlight and title colours individually. Tap Save theme to keep your changes. The QR button next to 🎨 lets you show your share code and QR to the caller at any time during the game — it is greyed out until cards are confirmed.
  • Single click/tap a number to mark it.
  • Double click/tap a number to undo it.
  • Click End Game to finish.
  • Any questions? Ask a fellow player!
  • Press and hold any button or label with a small ? indicator for a quick explanation of that feature.
